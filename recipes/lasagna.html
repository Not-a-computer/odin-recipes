Lasagna

This recipe will describe how to cook a simple, perfect, crispy lasagna. This is more of a relative guide on how I personally make lasagna, and the ingredients aren't perfectly measured.

For most of the ingredients, I just use an amount that I think will work.

Some of the ingredients are optional too, such as Worcestershire sauce, parmesan cheese and cream cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • 500g Mince
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Small Onion, 1/2 Large Onion
  • 2-4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 500g Pasata
  • Stick of Celary
  • A Carrot
  • 2-4 Button Mushroom
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Block of Cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
    • Cream Cheese

First, we will be creating the bolognese:

  1. The first step to your bolognese would be the vegtables. Dice all of the vegtables except the carrot which will be grated. With your diced mushrooms, add them to a pan with a small amount of water and fry them on a medium heat. I like to add a small bit of garlic granules and salt to them for taste.
  2. Once the mushrooms have had a decent amount of the moisture cooked out of them, store them for later use.
