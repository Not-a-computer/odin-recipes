Scrambled Eggs

This recipe isn't really a recipe, it is more of a technique but as I enjoy it so much I'd thought I'd add it.

This 'recipe' will describe how to make the most amazing and custardy scrambled eggs you have ever eaten.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter

Now we will be moving onto the technique, which is rather simple. All you will need is a pan.

  1. First, put your eggs into some sort of bowl, jug or container and beat them until they are mixed well together.
  2. Next, put the eggs in a cold pan, then put them on the stove on a medium-low heat and keep stirring the eggs.
  3. Once the eggs start to cook and start to become slightly solid, take the pan off the heat and keep stirring, and a small bit of butter prefferably cold, about half a teaspoon.
  4. Put the eggs back on the heat and keep repeating this process of: off the heat when it starts cooking, add butter, back on the heat. And remember to keep stirring.
  5. Do this until thhe egss become a similar texture to a custard, or until you like them.

After the eggs have cooked, I like to put them on some nice bread with avocado and hot sauce, and add salt and pepper to taste.
